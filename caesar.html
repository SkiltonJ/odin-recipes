Caesar's Health Salad

Description

A healthier take on a tradition caesar salad

Ingredients

  • A head of Cos lettuce
  • A bunch of Kale
  • A handful of walnuts
  • 200g chicken thighs
  • Cheese of your choosing (cheddar, parmasan, reggiano work best)
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Caesar dressing
  • Butter
  • Pepper

Method

  1. Rub chicken with Cajun seasoning, (get it in all nice and deep like)
  2. Add 50g butter to pan and place on medium heat
  3. Add chicken to pan, place another little bit of butter on top
  4. Cut up all veggies to desired size and place in a large bowl (You can rip the kale and lettuce)
  5. Cut walnuts into small pieces and place in the bowl
  6. When chicken is nearly finished cookin', place onto chopping board, cut to smaller pieces and place back in pan
  7. Move chicken around the pan to soak up all the butter and Cajun good good
  8. Switch stove off once chicken is cooked
  9. Place chicken into salad
  10. Grate cheese of choice over hot chicken
  11. Lather with caesar dressing
  12. Crack some pepper onto salad
  13. Munch like you have never munched before
