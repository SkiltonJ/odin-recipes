Jaenschy's Scrambly Bois

Description

Free Range scrambled eggs cooked with cheese and semi sundried tomoato.

*For best results serve on dense, seeded, dry toast and avocado.

Ingredients

  • 4 Free Range Eggs
  • 100g semi sun-dried tomatoes
  • Two pieces of Bergen bread
  • 1/2 an avocado
  • 50g grated/shredded cheese
  • As much sriracha mayo as you desire
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Method

  1. Put bread into toaster and push down
  2. Crack 4 eggs into a bowl
  3. Add grated cheese to eggs and whisk until aerated
  4. Cut up semi sun-dried tomatoes into thrids (they should already come in segments)
  5. Take toast out and smear avocado on top
  6. Place semi sun-dried tomatoes near the stove for easy reach
  7. Switch on heat and add oil to pan
  8. Wait 60 seconds until pan is hot and oil is runny
  9. Add eggs to pan (continuously move eggs so they do not stick to the pan)
  10. As eggs begin to take shape and look nearly done, add semi sun-dried tomatoes
  11. Give the eggs 10 seconds, flip to seal for another 10 seconds
  12. Take eggs off heat and scrape onto toaster
  13. Add salt, pepper, and healthy dose of Sriracha Mayo
  14. Enjoy!
