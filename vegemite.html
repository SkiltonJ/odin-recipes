Vegemite on toast

Description

My all time favourite meal, as a kid growing up in Australia, was the simple and salty goodness of butter and vegemite lathered on toast

Ingredients

  • Your choice of delicious bread
  • butter
  • Vegemite

Method

  1. Toast to desired level
  2. Stand eagerly at toaster to ensure a speedy extraction of toast
  3. Butter hot toast immediately
  4. Spread vegemite at desired rate
  5. Enjoy the salty delicious
